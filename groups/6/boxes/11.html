In selection locations on the Syracuse University campus, several blue boxes were set up in which any individual can write a note about what individuals can do to act forward following the Pan Am 103 tragedy. The Remembrance Scholars who set up the boxes discuss the process of putting the boxes together and the goal of the boxes.

Want to see the notes that were placed in the boxes? Click here to see the contributions.
