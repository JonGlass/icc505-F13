The Media



Steve Carlic, community engagement specialist at Syracuse Media Group, was one of the first Western reporters on the scene. Sean Kirst, a columnist at Syracuse Media Group, interviewed a woman named Marion Alderman of Camillus, who lost her daughter and son-in-law in the bombing, 10 years after the tragedy occurred. Finally, Melissa Chessher, an associate professor at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications, co-wrote "Looking for Lockerbie" with Newhouse photography professor Lawrence Mason. The three share their experiences reporting on Lockerbie, the journalistic lessons they took away from the experience and what the experience meant to them.
