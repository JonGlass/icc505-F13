This text box will explain the project: a tribute to the victims of the Pan-Am flight, focusing on their time in London. We will give a brief explanation of context - year, # of students, connection to Syracuse. We will also give instructions on how to go about navigating the different pages.

  • Tower Bridge

    The bridge, which is not the reference is the popular nursery rhyme "London Bridge is Falling Down," sits on the River Thames and is named so because of its proximity to the Tower of London. It was frequent for the students to take photos with the bridge in the background.

  • Her Majesty's Theatre

    Many of the students were in the SU theater program. They saw many shows in London, including Phantom of the Opera, The Last Temptation of Christ and The Public. Some drama students even critiquied the shows and told their peers which shows to see.
